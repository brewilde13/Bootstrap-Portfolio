About Me

Hi! My name is Brooke Wildermuth. Born and raised in Cincinnati (Ohio), Indiana University alumni, University of Michigan dieheart fan, and current Chicago resident - I cover all of the Midwest and don't anticipate leaving any time soon! I have 3 sisters (I know, my poor dad) and two loving parents. I currently work in the Finance industry as a consultant specializing in Operational Risk but have high hopes of switching career paths into a more fulfilling role.

Outside of work, I enjoy running, painting, enjoying the outdoors (skiing, hiking, biking), playing the piano and spending time with my family and friends. Some fun facts about me include:

  • 1) Of my three sisters, I am the only brunette. They are all bleach blonde (naturally) and my older sister used to convince me that I was adopted whenever she was mad (which was more often than not!)

  • 2) I punctured my spleen in the 3rd grade playing soccer.

  • 3) I eat a piece of chocolate almost every single day.
